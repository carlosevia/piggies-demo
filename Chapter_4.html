Chapter 4

The third little pig builds a strong house of bricks, which the Wolf cannot blow down.

Wolf Vs. third piggie

The two little piggies stormed into the third little piggies home. They told him all about the wolf that had tried to eat them! The little pig replied, "Everyone gets hungry, maybe he is really not so bad!"

The wolf was now very angry. He was still very hungry, and he smelled the most delicious smell coming from the third little piggies brick house. He thought to himself, "I have blown down two houses already, I bet I can blow down this house too!"

The wolf peered through the window and saw all three piggies together. He thought, "Yum! Appetizer, dinner, and dessert!"

The wolf knocked on the door and asked:

Little pig! Little pig! Let me in!

The piggies replied:

No! Not by the hairs of our chinny-chin-chin!

The wolf replied:

Then I'll huff, and I'll puff, and I will blow your house down!

The wolf huffed and puffed and the house didn't budge. He knew he may have to use a little more gusto. He huffed and puffed again, and the house still did not budge. The wolf became very angry.
